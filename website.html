The Hardest Goodbye

Shweta…People say college ends with memories, but for me, it ends with a heartbreak not because I'm leaving this place but because I'm leaving you behind.

You're not just my junior… you're my only reason to look back with tears in my eyes. The laughter we shared, the silent support, those random check-ins just to know you're okay that was my college life. And now it's time to walk away from it.

But listen to me carefully. I may not be there anymore, but if ever you feel lost, broken, or scared just call me, message me, whisper my name and I'll be there. Always. Because a brother's love doesn't graduate.

This world… it's not always kind. And I won't be there to protect you from every storm. But you, Shweta — you were born to fight. You were born to rise. So when the world gets cruel, don't bow down. burn brighter. Turn your pain into power. Let your tears turn into strength.

I've seen the innocence in your eyes, the kindness in your heart, the purity in your soul And it kills me to think this world might try to take that away. So I'm leaving now not because I want to but because I have to clear the path ahead. Make your way smoother. So that when it's your time to shine. there's nothing holding you back.

And when you feel alone, remember this Your big brother is always on your side. Maybe not in sight but always in spirit. Always in your corner. Always praying for your happiness.

So smile, Shweta. Be strong. Be bold. And when you rise — because I know you will make this world fall in love with that same heart that I'm so proud of today.

With all the love I'll ever carry,
Your Bhaiya ❤️

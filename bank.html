Knowledge Base

How to Create a New Account

To create a new account for a customer, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Account Management module
  2. Click on "Create New Account"
  3. Select the account type from the dropdown menu
  4. Choose the customer from the customer list
  5. Enter the initial deposit amount
  6. Select the currency for the account
  7. Click "Create Account" to complete the process

The system will automatically generate an account number and send a notification to the customer.

Processing Loan Applications

Loan application processing involves several steps:

  1. Verify customer information and credit history
  2. Review the loan application details
  3. Check the customer's debt-to-income ratio
  4. Assess collateral (if applicable)
  5. Determine the appropriate interest rate
  6. Approve or reject the application
  7. Notify the customer of the decision

For large loans, additional approval from a manager may be required.

Handling Transaction Disputes

When a customer disputes a transaction:

  1. Verify the customer's identity
  2. Review the transaction details
  3. Check for any signs of fraud
  4. Contact the merchant if necessary
  5. Initiate a chargeback if appropriate
  6. Keep the customer informed throughout the process
  7. Document all actions taken

Most disputes should be resolved within 10 business days.

Health & Wellness Tips

Balanced Nutrition

Focus on whole foods: fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Limit processed foods and added sugars for optimal health.

Regular Exercise

Aim for 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly. Include strength training twice a week for comprehensive fitness benefits.

Quality Sleep

7-9 hours nightly improves immunity, mood, and cognitive function. Maintain a consistent sleep schedule for best results.

Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration supports digestion, circulation, and temperature regulation.

Stress Management

Practice mindfulness, deep breathing, or yoga to reduce stress levels and improve overall well-being.

Avoid Harmful Habits

Eliminate smoking and limit alcohol consumption to significantly reduce risk of chronic diseases and improve longevity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment? +

For your first visit, please bring:

  • Photo ID and insurance card
  • List of current medications
  • Medical records from previous providers
  • Any relevant test results or imaging
  • A list of questions or concerns

What are your office hours? +

Our regular hours are:

  • Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

We offer extended hours for urgent care needs. Please call ahead for availability.

Do you accept my insurance? +

We participate with most major insurance plans including:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare
  • Medicaid
  • Cigna

Please contact our billing department to verify your specific coverage.

How do I request prescription refills? +

Prescription refill requests should be made through:

  • Our patient portal (preferred method)
  • By calling our prescription refill line
  • Through your pharmacy (they will contact us)

Please allow 48 hours for processing. Controlled substances require an office visit.

What COVID-19 precautions are in place? +

We continue to follow CDC guidelines to protect our patients and staff:

  • Enhanced cleaning protocols
  • Mask requirements when community levels are high
  • Social distancing in waiting areas
  • Telehealth options for appropriate visits
  • Vaccination and testing services available

How do I access my medical records? +

You can access your medical records through:

  • Our secure patient portal
  • By submitting a written request to our medical records department

There may be fees for printed copies as allowed by state law. Portal access is free.
